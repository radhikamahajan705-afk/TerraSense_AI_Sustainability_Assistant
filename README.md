# TerraSense_AI_Sustainability_Assistant
TerraSense AI is a Sustainability focused chatbot built with Botpress that educates users about climate change, energy conservation, water conservation, waste management and sustainable lifestyles in support of SDG 13: Climate Action

## Project Overview

TerraSense AI is a sustainability-focused chatbot developed using Botpress. The chatbot educates users about environmental sustainability through interactive conversations.

The chatbot provides information on climate change, energy conservation, water conservation, waste management, and sustainable lifestyles. The project supports United Nations Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) 13: Climate Action.

---

## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to increase environmental awareness and encourage sustainable habits by providing users with easy-to-understand sustainability information through a chatbot interface.

---

## Features

- Climate Change Awareness
- Energy Conservation Guidance
- Water Conservation Tips
- Waste Management Education
- Sustainable Lifestyle Recommendations
- Interactive Chatbot Experience
